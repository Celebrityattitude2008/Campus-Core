Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

shieldIntroduction

Welcome to Campus Core. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our platform.

databaseInformation We Collect

We collect information you provide when you:

  • Create an account (name, email address, student level)
  • Upload or download resources
  • Participate in discussions or use the chatbot
  • Make subscription payments

settingsHow We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our platform services
  • To personalize your academic experience
  • To send important announcements and updates
  • To process subscription payments securely
  • To ensure platform security and prevent abuse

lockData Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including Firebase Authentication, encrypted data transmission (HTTPS), and secure database rules to protect your information. We never sell your personal data to third parties.

contact_mailContact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at privacy@biu.edu.ng or visit our Support page.
